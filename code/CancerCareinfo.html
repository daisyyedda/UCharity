ABOUT

The Cancer Care Inc. (CCI) is a national non-profit organization dedicated to providing information, support, and resources to individuals affected by cancer. Cancer refers to a group of diseases characterized by uncontrolled cellular growth that may invade surrounding tissues and spread (metastasize) to other bodily tissues or organs. Established in 1944, Cancer Care Inc. is committed to offering counseling and emotional support with professional oncology social workers through private consultations, support groups, or one-on-one phone conferences; providing understandable information about cancer; and offering information concerning teleconferencing educational programs, seminars, and telephone support groups.
