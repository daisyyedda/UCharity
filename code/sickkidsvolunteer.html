About Opportunity

SickKids fight to make every kid a healthy kid, and have a direct stake in the lives and futures of children here and around the world. And we're looking for people who are far from ordinary to join us.

Opportunity Requirements

Post-secondary Student Volunteers
All volunteer programs in this category require you to be 18 years of age or older and currently enrolled in post-secondary education (or are of that age group). If you are over 18, then the SickKids Post-secondary Summer Student Volunteer Program could be for you!
